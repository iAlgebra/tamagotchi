TAMAGOTCHI

Your Tamagotchi pet starts off with 50 sleeping and eating points that decrease every second. Click on the START button to play! Good luck!

Rules:

  1. If the eating or sleeping points reach zero, your TAMAGOTCHI dies.
  2. Click on "ZZZ" to add 10 sleeping points to your TAMAGOTCHI. Max sleeping is 50 pts.
  3. Click on "Food" to feed your TAMAGOTCHI 10 points. If you feed your TAMAGOTCHI more than 50 points, it dies.
  4. If your Tamagotchi's age reaches a maximum age between 10 and 18, it dies.

Sleeping points: 50

Eating points: 50

Age: 0

Life Status: Alive
